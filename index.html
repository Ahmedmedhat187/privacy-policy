Privacy Policy

Noor Al-Quran (نور القرآن)

Effective date: 15 July 2026

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Noor Al-Quran is an offline-first Quran companion app. This policy explains what information the app handles, where it is stored, and the choices you have. In short: the app does not require an account, and it does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

The short version

  • No sign-up, no login, no user account.
  • Your data (bookmarks, memorization progress, playlists, settings, voice reflections) stays on your device. We never receive it.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no third-party trackers.
  • The only network activity is streaming reciter audio from a third-party host, and content you explicitly choose to share.

1. Who we are

Noor Al-Quran ("the app", "we", "us", or "our") is developed and published by Ahmed ("the developer"). This policy applies to the Noor Al-Quran mobile application distributed through the Google Play Store and any other platform where we publish it.

2. Information the app handles

2.1 Information you create in the app (stored only on your device)

As you use the app, it saves your activity locally so your experience persists between sessions. This may include:

  • Bookmarks and reading position in the Mushaf.
  • Memorization (Hifz) progress and daily goals.
  • Listening playlists and audio segments you build.
  • App preferences and settings (for example: language, theme, preferred reciter).
  • Voice reflections ("خواطر") you record on individual verses.

This information is stored in a private database on your device. It is not sent to us or to any server, and it is not linked to your identity.

2.2 Voice recordings

If you use the voice-reflection feature, the app records audio using your device microphone and stores those recordings locally on your device. Recordings are never uploaded to us or to any third party. You control when to record, and you can delete recordings from within the app.

2.3 Information collected automatically

We do not collect any usage analytics, device identifiers, advertising IDs, crash reports, or location data. The app contains no analytics or advertising software development kits (SDKs).

3. Where your data is stored

Noor Al-Quran is designed to work offline. The Quran text, translations, reciter metadata, and reference content are bundled inside the app itself. Everything you create is written to local databases kept in the app's private storage area on your device. Because this data never leaves your device, there is no cloud copy, no backup on our servers, and no way for us to access it.

4. Permissions the app requests

PermissionWhy it is used
Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) Only to record your voice reflections on verses. Recording happens only when you actively start it, and recordings are saved on your device.
Internet / Network access To stream reciter audio on demand and to share content you choose to share. The app functions offline for reading; network access is used only for streaming audio and sharing.

5. Third-party services

5.1 Audio streaming (EveryAyah)

When you play recited audio, the app streams audio files on demand from the third-party host everyayah.com. As with any request to a website, that host may automatically receive technical information required to deliver the audio, such as your device's IP address and standard request/log data. We do not control this host and do not receive this data. Your use of that service is subject to its own operator's practices.

5.2 Sharing (your device's share sheet)

When you choose to share a verse or card, the app hands the content to your device's built-in share sheet. Where it goes from there (a messaging app, email, social app, etc.) depends entirely on the app you select, and is governed by that app's privacy policy.

5.3 Google Play

The app is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information related to app installation and delivery under its own policies. See the Google Privacy Policy.

6. What we do not do

  • We do not require or offer an account, and we do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect your location.

7. Data retention & deletion

Because all of your data is stored locally, you are in full control of it:

  • Delete individual items (such as bookmarks or voice recordings) from within the app.
  • Clear the app's data from your device's system settings (Settings → Apps → Noor Al-Quran → Storage → Clear data).
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data it created on your device.

We retain no personal data of yours, because we never receive any. There is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

8. Children's privacy

Noor Al-Quran is suitable for a general audience, including children. The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children because we do not collect personal data at all.

9. Data security

Your data resides in the app's private storage on your own device and is protected by your device's operating-system security model. Because we do not transmit or store your data on external servers, it is not exposed to the risks associated with cloud storage. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Because Noor Al-Quran does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our systems, we hold no personal data about you to access, export, or erase. You can exercise full control over the data on your device using the steps in section 7. If you have questions about your rights, contact us using the details below.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time — for example, if we add features that change how data is handled. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where the change is significant, provide a more prominent notice. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
